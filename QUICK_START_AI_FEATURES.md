# 🚀 Quick Start: AI Intelligence Features

## 🎯 What's New?

WanderMood now has **AI-powered intelligence** that learns from you and gets smarter over time!

---

## 🆕 New User Flow

### 1️⃣ Check In with Moody
**Location:** Moody Hub → "Check in" button

**What happens:**
- Tell Moody about your day (type or select quick options)
- Choose your mood and activities
- Moody responds contextually to what you wrote
- **NEW:** Moody remembers previous conversations!

**Example:**
```
You: "Just tried on some new clothes, feeling tired"
Moody: "Ooh new clothes! 👗 I love that energy! Sounds like you need some chill time 😴"
```

---

### 2️⃣ Rate Your Activities
**Location:** Automatically appears after check-in OR swipe on timeline activities

**What happens:**
- Beautiful rating interface slides up
- Rate with stars (1-5)
- Select what you loved (The vibe, The food, etc.)
- Toggle "Would recommend"
- Add optional notes

**Why it matters:**
- Moody learns your preferences
- Gets smarter recommendations
- Builds your weekly reflection

---

### 3️⃣ View Weekly Reflection
**Location:** Moody Hub (shows automatically on Mondays)

**What you see:**
- Activities completed this week
- New places you tried
- Your dominant mood
- Mood journey visualization
- Achievements unlocked
- Top-rated experiences
- Pattern insights

**Example Achievements:**
- "Completed 5 activities! 🎉"
- "Tried 3 new places! 🗺️"
- "Loved 4 experiences! ❤️"

## 🧠 How Moody Learns

### After Each Rating:
1. **Mood-Activity Scores** are calculated
   - "When I'm adventurous, I love hiking"
   - Score: 0.85 (very positive correlation)

2. **Tag Frequencies** are tracked
   - "The vibe" mentioned 15 times
   - Your most important factor!

3. **Time Preferences** are noted
   - You rate morning activities 30% higher
   - Moody suggests morning plans more often

4. **Pattern Recognition** updates
   - Learns what you consistently love
   - Identifies your style

## 🎯 Pro Tips

### For Best Results:

1. **Check in daily**
   - More data = smarter Moody
   - Builds better patterns

2. **Rate honestly**
   - Don't just 5-star everything
   - Honest ratings = better suggestions

3. **Use tags**
   - Helps Moody understand what matters
   - "The vibe" vs "The food"

4. **Add notes sometimes**
   - Qualitative feedback is gold
   - Moody learns from your words

5. **Complete activities**
   - Swipe to complete on timeline
   - Rate right after experiencing

## 🔑 Key Interactions

### In Moody Hub:
- Tap **"Check in"** → Start check-in flow
- Tap **Moody character** → Open chat
- Tap **timeline period** → View activities
- Swipe **activity right** → Complete & rate
- Tap **reflection card** → View full reflection

### During Check-in:
- Type freely → Moody reads and responds
- Select mood card → Animates and selects
- Toggle reactions → Add emoji reactions
- Tap send → Submits and shows ratings

### In Rating Sheet:
- Tap star → Animated selection
- Tap tag → Toggle selection
- Toggle recommend → Marks preference
- Type notes → Optional detailed feedback
- Tap submit → Saves and celebrates
